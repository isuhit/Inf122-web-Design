Unit 1: Form Structural Architecture

When collecting data, clarity is key. HTML5 uses specific tags to group related fields logically so users (and screen readers) don't get confused.

  • <fieldset>: Groups related elements together inside a visual box.
  • <legend>: Acts as a caption or title for the fieldset container.
